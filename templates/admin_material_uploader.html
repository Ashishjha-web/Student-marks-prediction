CSV/Excel Upload Guidelines
Required Column Structure

For the analytics model to work correctly, your data file (CSV or Excel) must contain exactly 16 columns in the following order:

  1. Previous Semester Subject 1 Marks (External, out of 70)
  2. Previous Semester Subject 2 Marks (External, out of 70)
  3. Previous Semester Subject 3 Marks (External, out of 70)
  4. Previous Semester Subject 4 Marks (External, out of 70)
  5. Previous Semester Subject 5 Marks (External, out of 70)
  6. Current Semester Subject 1 Class Test Average (out of 20)
  7. Current Semester Subject 2 Class Test Average (out of 20)
  8. Current Semester Subject 3 Class Test Average (out of 20)
  9. Current Semester Subject 4 Class Test Average (out of 20)
  10. Current Semester Subject 5 Class Test Average (out of 20)
  11. Previous Semesters' Average Attendance (in %)
  12. Final Current Semester Subject 1 Marks (External, out of 70)
  13. Final Current Semester Subject 2 Marks (External, out of 70)
  14. Final Current Semester Subject 3 Marks (External, out of 70)
  15. Final Current Semester Subject 4 Marks (External, out of 70)
  16. Final Current Semester Subject 5 Marks (External, out of 70)
Note: The system will automatically name the uploaded file based on the Branch and Semester you select on the upload form. You do not need to name the file yourself.
