Analytics Data Upload Guide
Easy 3-Step Process

To ensure 100% accuracy and avoid errors, please follow these simple steps:

  • Select Branch & Semester: On the upload form, choose the correct Branch and Semester for the data you wish to upload.
  • Download the Template: A "Download Template" button will become active. Click it to download a CSV file that is perfectly formatted with the correct subject IDs for your selection.
  • Fill & Upload: Open the downloaded template, fill in the rows with your student data, save the file, and then upload it using the form.
Why Use the Template?
The template automatically includes the 16 required columns with the correct internal IDs (e.g., `dbms_ct`, `os_final`). This prevents formatting errors and ensures your data works perfectly with our prediction model.
