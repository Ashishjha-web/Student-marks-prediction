Top 5 Study Habits of High-Achieving Students

Stop studying harder—start studying smarter. Top students don't have more time; they use it more effectively. Unlock your potential with these five proven study habits:

  1. Active Recall: Don't just re-read. Close the book and actively recall information to make it stick.
  2. Spaced Repetition: Beat the forgetting curve by reviewing material in smart, spaced-out intervals.
  3. The Feynman Technique: If you can't explain a concept simply, you don't understand it. Teach it to learn it.
  4. Interleaving: Mix up your subjects during study sessions to build stronger mental connections.
  5. Elaboration: Go beyond memorization by asking "how" and "why" to connect new ideas to what you already know.

Master these techniques to learn faster and achieve more.
