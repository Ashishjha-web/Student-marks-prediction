Your Trust, Secured by Design

We consider your data's security to be paramount. Our security isn't an afterthought; it's built into our core architecture. Here's how we protect your trust:

  • End-to-End Encryption: Your data is protected with AES-256 encryption both in transit and at rest—the same standard used by banks and governments.
  • Advanced Password Hashing: We never store plaintext passwords. All credentials are secured using the industry-standard bcrypt hashing algorithm.
  • Role-Based Access Control (RBAC): Our granular permission system ensures users can only access the data relevant to their role, guaranteeing student privacy.
  • Continuous Threat Monitoring: Our infrastructure is monitored 24/7 for vulnerabilities and suspicious activity, keeping us a step ahead of potential threats.
